About Us

The Arcade started as a hobby project: a small coding playground where I can experiment, learn, and share simple interactive browser experiences. Many of the games and tools here are experiments created while testing ideas or learning new programming concepts. The name The Arcade is inspired by the arcade cabinets of earlier decades, where you would walk up to a machine and choose which game to play. In the same way, this website acts like a digital hallway of cabinets—each page is a different project you can explore. The site is designed to be lightweight, accessible on desktop and mobile devices, and fast-loading. Alongside simple arcade-style games, you may find small tools, visual experiments, or other coding projects that seemed interesting to build at the time. The Arcade is meant to be fun, experimental, and casual. If you enjoy simple games, retro inspiration, or small coding projects, this is the place to explore. But the most important thing is to not look at the source code. I CANNOT stress this enough, it's very... interesting to say the least

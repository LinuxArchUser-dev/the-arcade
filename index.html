Welcome To The Arcade

The Arcade is a simple browser-based collection of classic-style games and small coding projects you can play instantly with no downloads required. Everything runs directly in your browser, so you can jump in and start playing within seconds. The goal is to recreate the feeling of old-school arcade machines with simple mechanics, pixel-style graphics, and fast, fun gameplay. Whether you're trying to beat your high score in Flap the Square or testing your reflexes in a quick challenge, there’s always something fun to try. Open a page, pick a game, and start playing—just like walking up to an arcade cabinet. New games and small projects are added over time as the site grows.
